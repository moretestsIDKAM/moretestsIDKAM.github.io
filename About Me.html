About Me

Here is some information about me.

I want to be an animator in the near future. I've

had an intrest in the feild of animation ever since

I was ten after watching a segment on how animation for

the Rugrats in Paris movie was made. I'm currently an

Animation and Illustration major studying at San Jose

State University.

Artsit Statement

As an artist I like to play with color especially gradients

because the smooth transition between colors is such an

apealing sight to me. I like to combine texture and

stylization into my works aswell. When I have time to I

like to draw characters, and draft out stories to use these

characters in. Lately I've been drawing alot of fan art in my

free time since it's just nice to play with existing

characters from time to time.
