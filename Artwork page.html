Gemstone Light Show Room

"Gemstone Light Show Room" is a work made using

p5.js. At first I had only floating gems but

I wanted to disply them nicely. So I recreated

a light room that resembled a James Turrell's

work.I combined parts from codes in p5.js's

refrence page and modified them until I was

satisfied with the result. I even made the gem

textures myself using the drawing program Artrage.

One can play with the lighting on p5.js's web

editor.Click this image to open the link.

Charlotte

"Charlotte" is a work made using bristol paper

and tradtional inks. I made this for my ANI 112A

class in which I was tasked to design a character

for an adaptation of an urban legend. My story was

"Spider Bite" and designed a main character.

I also spiced things up by having the story take

place in the 1980s(hence the fashion) while useing

an artsyle from the 1920s to 1930s.

Charlotte page 1

"Charlotte page 1" is a work made using bristol paper

and tradtional inks for my ANI 112A class. I

was tasked with making a comic adaptaion of my

chosen urban legend using the style and character

I designed.This first page introduces Charlotte

and her misbehaviors. I wanted to give the comic a

"animated short from the 20s that would unsettle

people online due to the horrific turn at the end."

feel, so I used watered down ink to mimic water

color and cartoon hummor via a semi annoyed

narrator.
